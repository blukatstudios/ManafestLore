History

The Parthians earned a name for themselves far before the divines came to the overworld. Refugees from a war in the dream realm sought refuge in the Parthian empire. These dreamers brought with them a wealth of knowledge of the arcane arts, which they shared openly with their protectors. Parthian mages provided economic, industrial, and military power that neighboring regions simply lacked. It's this advantage that put Persia on the world map.

Government and Politics

Persia is an absolute monarchy at the federal level, but has semi-autonomous provinces governed by satraps at lower levels of authority. After magic turned Persia into a regional superpower, Satraps were assigned to the localities of each province, serving as mayors. Despite the autonomy of the Satraps, they all answer to the king and provide the king's administration with tribute. The professional army is hosted from the federal level, and will enforce the king's will.
